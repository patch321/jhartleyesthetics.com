Tips For a Successful Visit

  • Exfoliating a few day before your wax and in between appointments will help prevent ingrown hairs.
  • Avoid tanning 24 hours before and after your wax.
  • Avoid being in a hot tub 24-48 hours after your wax.
  • The wax area may be irritated for anywhere from 30 minutes to a full day after the wax, depending on the area waxed
  • I do not perform brazilians or bikini waxes on women who are menstruating. During menstruation, blood flow is increased which causes it to be more uncomfortable and stay irritated for a longer period of time.
